Present Perfect Tense: Tushuntirish

Hozirgi Tugallangan Zamonni oson o'rganing!

Nima uchun Present Perfect Tense?

Present Perfect (Hozirgi Tugallangan Zamon) ingliz tilida juda muhim zamonlardan biridir. U o'tmishda boshlangan va hozirgi paytga bog'liq bo'lgan harakatlar yoki holatlarni ifodalash uchun ishlatiladi. Keling, buni batafsil ko'rib chiqamiz!

Asosiy Qoidalar 

1. Harakatning Natijasi Hozirgi Paytga Tegishli

O'tmishda sodir bo'lgan harakatning hozirgi paytdagi natijasi yoki oqibati muhim bo'lsa.

Misol: I have lost my keys. (Men kalitlarimni yo'qotdim. - Hozirda ularni topa olmayapman.)

2. Hayotiy Tajriba (Experience)

Hozirgi paytgacha hayotingizda sodir bo'lgan tajribalar haqida gapirganda. Ko'pincha "ever" (biror marta) va "never" (hech qachon) bilan ishlatiladi.

Misol: She has never been to London. (U hech qachon Londonda bo'lmagan.)

3. Hozirgi Paytgacha Davom Etgan Harakatlar

O'tmishda boshlangan va hozirgi paytgacha davom etayotgan harakatlar yoki holatlar. Ko'pincha "for" (davomiylik) va "since" (boshlanish nuqtasi) bilan ishlatiladi.

Misol: They have lived here for five years. (Ular bu yerda besh yildan beri yashayaptilar.)

Misol: He has worked since 2010. (U 2010 yildan beri ishlayapti.)

4. Yaqinda Tugallangan Harakatlar

Yaqinda tugallangan va natijasi sezilarli bo'lgan harakatlar. Ko'pincha "just" (hozirgina) bilan ishlatiladi.

Misol: I have just finished my homework. (Men hozirgina uy vazifamni tugatdim.)

Strukturalar 

Present Perfect zamonining gap tuzilishi quyidagicha:

Darak Gap (Affirmative)

Ega (Subject) + have / has + Fe'lning 3-shakli (Past Participle)

Misol: I have finished. (Men tugatdim.)

Misol: She has seen. (U ko'rgan.)

Inkor Gap (Negative)

Ega (Subject) + have / has + not + Fe'lning 3-shakli (Past Participle)

Misol: We have not eaten. (Biz ovqatlanmadik.)

Misol: He has not arrived yet. (U hali kelmadi.)

So'roq Gap (Interrogative)

Have / Has + Ega (Subject) + Fe'lning 3-shakli (Past Participle)?

Misol: Have you read this book? (Siz bu kitobni o'qiganmisiz?)

Misol: Has she called you? (U sizga qo'ng'iroq qilganmi?)

Kalit So'zlar (Keywords) 

Present Perfect zamoni bilan tez-tez ishlatiladigan ba'zi kalit so'zlar:

  • Just: hozirgina, yaqinda
  • Already: allaqachon
  • Yet: hali (inkor va so'roq gaplarda)
  • Never: hech qachon
  • Ever: biror marta (so'roq gaplarda)
  • For: davomida (muddatni bildiradi)
  • Since: dan beri (boshlanish nuqtasini bildiradi)
  • Recently: yaqinda
  • Lately: so'nggi paytlarda
  • So far: hozirgacha
  • This week/month/year: bu hafta/oy/yil

Misollar 

I have finished my project.
(Men loyihamni tugatdim.)

(Natija muhim: loyiha tayyor.)

She has lived in New York for 10 years.
(U Nyu-Yorkda 10 yildan beri yashaydi.)

(Hozirgacha davom etayotgan harakat.)

Have you ever eaten sushi?
(Siz biror marta sushi yeganmisiz?)

(Hayotiy tajriba.)

They haven't seen that movie yet.
(Ular bu filmni hali ko'rishmadi.)

(Hali sodir bo'lmagan harakat.)

He has just left the office.
(U hozirgina ofisdan chiqib ketdi.)

(Yaqinda tugallangan harakat.)

We have known each other since childhood.
(Biz bolaligimizdan beri bir-birimizni bilamiz.)

(Boshlanish nuqtasi.)
