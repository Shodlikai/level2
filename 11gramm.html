Question Tags: Tushuntirish

Savol-taglarni (Question Tags) oson o'rganing!

Nima uchun Question Tags?

Question Tag (Savol-tag) ingliz tilida gap oxiriga qo'shiladigan qisqa savoldir. U suhbatdoshdan tasdiqlash olish yoki fikrni so'rash uchun ishlatiladi. Ko'pincha o'zbek tilidagi "shunday emasmi?", "to'g'rimi?" kabi ifodalarga mos keladi. Keling, buni batafsil ko'rib chiqamiz!

Asosiy Qoidalar 

1. Asosiy Gap va Tagning Qarama-qarshi Bo'lishi

Agar asosiy gap darak gap bo'lsa (ijobiy), tag inkor bo'ladi. Agar asosiy gap inkor gap bo'lsa (salbiy), tag darak bo'ladi.

Misol: You are happy, aren't you? (Siz xursandsiz, shunday emasmi?)

Misol: She isn't here, is she? (U bu yerda emas, shundaymi?)

2. To'g'ri Yordamchi Fe'lni Ishlatish

Question Tagda asosiy gapdagi yordamchi fe'l (be, have, do, will, can, must, etc.) ishlatiladi.

Misol: They can swim, can't they? (Ular suza oladi, shunday emasmi?)

Misol: He has finished, hasn't he? (U tugatdi, shunday emasmi?)

3. 'Do/Does/Did' Ishlatish

Agar asosiy gapda yordamchi fe'l bo'lmasa (Simple Present yoki Simple Past zamonlarida), tagda 'do', 'does' yoki 'did' ishlatiladi.

Misol: You play tennis, don't you? (Siz tennis o'ynaysiz, shunday emasmi?)

Misol: She went home, didn't she? (U uyga ketdi, shunday emasmi?)

4. Kesimning (Subject) Olmosh Bo'lishi

Question Tagda har doim kesimga mos olmosh ishlatiladi.

Misol: Tom is kind, isn't he? (Tom mehribon, shunday emasmi?)

Misol: Your parents are here, aren't they? (Ota-onangiz shu yerda, shunday emasmi?)

Strukturalar 

Question Tagning umumiy tuzilishi quyidagicha:

Darak Gap + Inkor Tag

Asosiy Gap (Positive) + Yordamchi Fe'l (negativ shaklda) + Olmosh?

Misol: You are happy, aren't you?

Misol: He works hard, doesn't he?

Inkor Gap + Darak Tag

Asosiy Gap (Negative) + Yordamchi Fe'l (pozitiv shaklda) + Olmosh?

Misol: They aren't ready, are they?

Misol: She didn't call, did she?

Istisnolar (Exceptions) 

Ba'zi gaplar va fe'llar uchun Question Taglar o'ziga xos tarzda shakllanadi:

1. I am...

Gap "I am..." bilan boshlansa, tag "aren't I?" bo'ladi.

Misol: I am late, aren't I?

2. Let's...

"Let's..." bilan boshlangan takliflar uchun tag "shall we?" bo'ladi.

Misol: Let's go, shall we?

3. Buyruqlar (Imperatives)

Buyruq gaplar uchun "will you?", "won't you?", "can you?", "could you?" ishlatilishi mumkin.

Misol: Open the door, will you?

Misol: Don't forget, will you?

4. Salbiy Ma'noli So'zlar

Agar asosiy gapda salbiy ma'no beruvchi so'zlar bo'lsa (masalan: Nothing, Nobody, No one, Hardly, Seldom, Barely, Scarcely), tag darak bo'ladi.

Misol: Nothing happened, did it?

Misol: Nobody called, did they?

Misol: He hardly ever goes out, does he?

5. This/That/These/Those

Agar asosiy gapda `this/that` bo'lsa, tagda `it` ishlatiladi. Agar `these/those` bo'lsa, `they` ishlatiladi.

Misol: This is your book, isn't it?

Misol: Those are your keys, aren't they?

6. There is/are...

Agar asosiy gap "There is/are..." bilan boshlansa, tagda "there" ishlatiladi.

Misol: There is a problem, isn't there?

Misol: There are many people, aren't there?

7. Everyone/Somebody/Nobody/Anyone...

Bu olmoshlar uchun tagda ko'plik olmoshi "they" ishlatiladi.

Misol: Everyone is here, aren't they?

Misol: Nobody arrived, did they?
