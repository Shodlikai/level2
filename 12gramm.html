Present Perfect Continuous Tense

Hozirgi Tugallangan Davomli Zamonni o'rganing!

Present Perfect Continuous Nima?

Present Perfect Continuous (Hozirgi Tugallangan Davomli Zamon) o'tmishda boshlangan va hozirgi paytda ham davom etayotgan yoki yaqindagina tugallangan harakatlarni ifodalash uchun ishlatiladi. Bu zamon harakatning **davomiyligiga** va uning hozirgi payt bilan bog'liqligiga urg'u beradi.

Eslatma: Bu zamon ko'pincha **"Since"** (dan beri) va **"For"** (davomida) bilan birga ishlatiladi, chunki ular harakatning qancha vaqt davom etganini yoki qachon boshlanganini ko'rsatadi.

Strukturalar 

Present Perfect Continuous Tense ning gap tuzilishi quyidagicha:

Darak Gap (Affirmative)

Ega (Subject) + have / has been + Fe'l + -ing

Misol: I have been studying for three hours. (Men uch soatdan beri o'qiyapman.)

Misol: She has been working all day. (U butun kun ishlayapti.)

Inkor Gap (Negative)

Ega (Subject) + have / has not been (yoki haven't / hasn't been) + Fe'l + -ing

Misol: We haven't been waiting long. (Biz uzoq kutmadik.)

Misol: He hasn't been sleeping well. (U yaxshi uxlayotgani yo'q.)

So'roq Gap (Interrogative)

Have / Has + Ega (Subject) + been + Fe'l + -ing?

Misol: Have you been crying? (Siz yig'layapsizmi?)

Misol: Has he been living here since 2010? (U 2010 yildan beri shu yerda yashayaptimi?)

Asosiy Ishlatilish Holatlari 

1. O'tmishda Boshlanib, Hozirda Ham Davom Etayotgan Harakatlar

Bu eng keng tarqalgan ishlatilish holati. Harakat o'tmishda boshlangan va gapirilayotgan paytda ham davom etayotgan bo'ladi.

Misol: It has been raining all morning. (Butun ertalab yomg'ir yog'ayapti.) - Hozir ham yog'yapti.

Misol: I have been learning English for five years. (Men besh yildan beri ingliz tili o'rganayapman.) - Hozir ham o'rganyapman.

2. Yaqindagina Tugallangan Harakatlar, Natijasi Hozir Ko'rinib Turgan

Harakat endigina tugagan, lekin uning natijasi yoki oqibati hozirgi paytda aniq ko'rinadi.

Misol: Your eyes are red. Have you been crying? (Ko'zlaringiz qizargan. Yig'layapsizmi?)

Misol: The ground is wet. It has been raining. (Yer ho'l. Yomg'ir yog'gan.)

3. Vaqtga Urg'u Berish (Since / For / How long)

**"Since"** (qachondan beri), **"For"** (qancha vaqt davomida) va **"How long"** (qancha vaqt) so'zlari bilan birga harakatning davomiyligiga urg'u berish uchun ishlatiladi.

Misol: We have been living here since 2020. (Biz 2020 yildan beri shu yerda yashayapmiz.)

Misol: He has been watching TV for two hours. (U ikki soatdan beri televizor ko'rayapti.)

Misol: How long have you been waiting? (Qancha vaqtdan beri kutyapsiz?)

"Since", "For" va "How long" bilan PPC 

Bu kalit so'zlar Present Perfect Continuous zamoni bilan juda tez-tez ishlatiladi va harakatning vaqtini aniqlashga yordam beradi.

"Since" (dan beri)

Harakatning **boshlanish nuqtasini** bildiradi (o'tmishdagi aniq bir vaqt yoki voqea).

Misol: She has been teaching English since 2005. (U 2005 yildan beri ingliz tili o'qitmoqda.)

Misol: I have been feeling unwell since I woke up. (Uyg'onganimdan beri o'zimni yaxshi his qilmayapman.)

"For" (davomida)

Harakatning **davomiyligini (muddatini)** bildiradi (vaqt oralig'i).

Misol: They have been building that house for six months. (Ular bu uyni olti oydan beri qurmoqda.)

Misol: He has been talking on the phone for ages. (U ancha vaqtdan beri telefonda gaplashmoqda.)

"How long?" (qancha vaqt?)

Harakatning qancha vaqtdan beri davom etayotganini so'rash uchun ishlatiladi.

Misol: How long have you been studying? (Qancha vaqtdan beri o'qiyapsiz?)

Misol: How long has she been waiting? (U qancha vaqtdan beri kutyapti?)

PPC vs. Present Perfect Simple 

Ikkala zamon ham o'tmish va hozirgi paytni bog'lasa-da, farqlari bor:

Xususiyat Present Perfect Continuous Present Perfect Simple
Asosiy e'tibor Harakatning **davomiyligi** yoki takrorlanishi Harakatning **tugallanganligi** yoki natijasi
Harakat holati Davom etmoqda / Yaqindagina tugagan Tugallangan
Misol (davomiylik) I have been reading for two hours. (Hali ham o'qiyapman.) I have read two books. (Tugallangan miqdor.)
Misol (natija) The road is wet because it has been raining. (Yog'ish davomida yog'gan.) It has rained a lot this month. (Umumiy fakt, miqdorga urg'u.)

Eslatma: Ba'zi "statik" fe'llar (state verbs - bilish, sevish, egalik qilish kabi) Continuous zamonlarda ishlatilmaydi. Ular bilan Present Perfect Simple ishlatiladi: I have known him for years. (NOT: I have been knowing him...)

Mashqlar 

1. Bo'sh Joylarni To'ldiring (Present Perfect Continuous)

Qavs ichidagi fe'llarni Present Perfect Continuous zamonida to'g'ri shaklda yozing.

1. She _____ (work) in the garden all morning.

2. I _____ (study) English for five years.

3. They _____ (not / wait) long.

4. _____ you _____ (sleep) well recently?

5. He _____ (cook) since 5 PM, and the dinner is almost ready.

2. "Since" yoki "For" ni Tanlang

Har bir gap uchun "Since" yoki "For" dan to'g'risini tanlang.

6. It has been raining _____ two hours.

7. I have been learning German _____ I was ten years old.

8. They have been living in this house _____ a long time.

9. She hasn't been feeling well _____ last Monday.

3. "How long?" So'roq Gapini Tuzing

Berilgan gapga mos "How long?" so'rog'ini tuzing.

10. (He / work / here / for ten years)

11. (You / learn / English / since 2020)

4. Gapni Qayta Tartiblang (Drag & Drop)

So'zlardan Present Perfect Continuous zamonida to'g'ri gap tuzing.

12. have / been / they / waiting / for / an hour

have been they waiting for an hour

13. has / it / all / been / raining / day

has it all been raining day

5. Present Perfect Continuous yoki Present Perfect Simple?

Qavs ichidagi fe'lni to'g'ri zamonda (PPC yoki PPS) yozing.

14. I _____ (read) this book for two hours, and I'm still on page 50.

15. She _____ (write) three letters this morning.

16. How many times _____ you _____ (visit) London?

17. We _____ (live) in this city since 2010. (emphasize duration)

18. I _____ (know) him for ages. (State verb)
