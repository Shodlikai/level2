Quantifiers: Each, Every, Both, Neither, Either, None

Miqdor bildiruvchi so'zlar va ularning ishlatilishi!

Miqdor Bildiruvchi So'zlar Nima?

Miqdor bildiruvchi so'zlar (Quantifiers) otlardan oldin kelib, ularning miqdorini yoki sonini ifodalaydi. Ingliz tilida "Each", "Every", "Both", "Neither", "Either" va "None" kabi so'zlar tez-tez ishlatiladi va ularning har birining o'ziga xos qoidalari bor. Bu so'zlarni to'g'ri ishlatish jumlalarning ma'nosini aniq ifodalash uchun juda muhimdir.

Both (Ikkalasi ham) ü§ù

"Both" ikki narsa yoki ikki kishiga murojaat qilish uchun ishlatiladi. Bu "birinchi va ikkinchisi ham" degan ma'noni anglatadi.

Ishlatilish Holatlari:

1. Ot bilan: "Both + Plural Noun"

Both + ko'plikdagi ot

Misol: Both students are intelligent. (Ikkala talaba ham aqlli.)

2. Olmosh bilan: "Both of + Plural Pronoun / Determinant + Plural Noun"

Both of + ko'plikdagi olmosh (us, them) / aniqlovchi + ko'plikdagi ot

Misol: Both of us are tired. (Ikkalamiz ham charchadik.)

Misol: Both of my parents are doctors. (Mening ota-onamning ikkalasi ham shifokor.)

3. Olmoshning o'zi sifatida (Pronoun): "Pronoun (plural) + both"

Ko'plikdagi olmosh + both

Misol: We both love pizza. (Ikkalamiz ham pitsani yaxshi ko'ramiz.)

Misol: They both passed the exam. (Ularning ikkalasi ham imtihondan o'tishdi.)

4. "Both...and..." strukturasi

Ikkita narsa yoki fikrni birga bog'lash uchun ishlatiladi.

Both ... and ...

Misol: She speaks both English and Spanish. (U ham ingliz, ham ispan tilida gapiradi.)

Misol: Both John and Mary are coming to the party. (Jon ham, Meri ham bazmga kelyapti.)

Muhim Qoida: "Both" har doim ko'plik fe'l bilan ishlatiladi. "Both" inkor gaplarda ishlatilmaydi; uning o'rniga "Neither" ishlatiladi.

Xato: Both of them are not here. (Ikkalasiham shu yerda emas.)

To'g'ri: Neither of them is here. (Ularning hech biri shu yerda emas.)

Each (Har biri / Har bir) üë•

"Each" ikki yoki undan ortiq odam yoki narsaga murojaat qiladi, ammo ularning har birini alohida-alohida hisoblaydi. Bu individualizmga urg'u beradi.

Ishlatilish Holatlari:

1. Ot bilan: "Each + Singular Noun"

Each + birlikdagi ot

Misol: Each student received a certificate. (Har bir talaba sertifikat oldi.)

2. "Each of + Determiner + Plural Noun / Pronoun (plural object)"

Each of + aniqlovchi + ko'plikdagi ot / ko'plikdagi olmosh

Misol: Each of the books costs $10. (Kitoblarning har biri 10 dollar turadi.)

Misol: Each of us has a different opinion. (Har birimizning fikrimiz har xil.)

3. Olmoshning o'zi sifatida (Pronoun): "Pronoun (plural subject) + each"

Ko'plikdagi olmoshdan keyin kelib, guruhdagi har bir a'zoga taalluqli ekanligini bildiradi.

We / You / They + each + fe'l

Misol: We each paid for our own dinner. (Har birimiz o'z kechki ovqatimiz uchun to'ladik.)

Misol: They each have a unique talent. (Ularning har birida noyob iste'dod bor.)

Muhim Qoidalar:

  • "Each" har doim birlik fe'l bilan ishlatiladi.
  • "Each" inkor gaplarda to'g'ridan-to'g'ri ishlatilmaydi, uning o'rniga "None of" ishlatiladi.
  • "Almost", "Nearly", "Not" kabi so'zlardan keyin "Each" emas, balki "Every" ishlatiladi.

Xato: Each of them didn't come. (Ularning har biri kelmadi.)

To'g'ri: None of them came. (Ularning hech biri kelmadi.)

Xato: Almost each student passed. (Deyarli har bir talaba o'tdi.)

To'g'ri: Almost every student passed. (Deyarli har bir talaba o'tdi.)

Every (Har bir / Har qaysi) üåê

"Every" uch yoki undan ortiq odam yoki narsaga murojaat qiladi va guruhni **bir butun** sifatida ko'rsatadi. Bu umumiy holatga urg'u beradi.

Ishlatilish Holatlari:

1. Ot bilan: "Every + Singular Noun"

Every + birlikdagi ot

Misol: Every car has four wheels. (Har bir mashinaning to'rtta g'ildiragi bor.)

Misol: Every day is a new beginning. (Har bir kun yangi boshlanishdir.)

Muhim Qoidalar:

  • "Every" har doim birlik fe'l bilan ishlatiladi.
  • "Every" dan keyin **"of"** ishlatilmaydi.
  • "Almost", "Nearly", "Not" kabi so'zlar bilan birga ishlatilishi mumkin.

Xato: Every of the students passed. (Talabalarning har biri o'tdi.)

To'g'ri: Every student passed. (Har bir talaba o'tdi.)

To'g'ri: Not every student passed. (Har bir talaba ham o'tmadi.)

Each vs. Every: Taqqoslash ‚öñÔ∏è

Ikkala so'z ham "har bir" degan ma'noni bersa-da, ularning o'ziga xos farqlari bor:

Xususiyat Each Every
E'tibor Guruhdagi **har bir a'zoni alohida-alohida** Guruhni **bir butun sifatida**
Son Odatda kichik, aniq guruhlar; ikki yoki undan ortiq Katta, noaniq guruhlar; uch yoki undan ortiq
"Of" bilan Ishlatiladi ("Each of the...") Ishlatilmaydi
Inkor gapda To'g'ridan-to'g'ri inkor qilinmaydi (None of ishlatiladi) Ishlatiladi ("Not every...")
"Almost/Nearly" bilan Ishlatilmaydi (Every ishlatiladi) Ishlatiladi
Misol Each student has a pen. Every student has a pen.

Neither (Hech biri / Na ... na) üö´

"Neither" ikki narsa yoki ikki kishiga nisbatan "ikkalasidan ham hech biri emas" degan ma'noni anglatadi. "Both" ning inkoridir.

Ishlatilish Holatlari:

1. Aniqlovchi sifatida: "Neither + Singular Noun"

Neither + birlikdagi ot

Misol: Neither answer is correct. (Ikkala javob ham to'g'ri emas.)

2. Olmosh sifatida: "Neither of + Determiner + Plural Noun / Pronoun (plural object)"

Neither of + aniqlovchi + ko'plikdagi ot / ko'plikdagi olmosh

Misol: Neither of the students passed the exam. (Talabalarning hech biri imtihondan o'tmadi.)

Misol: Neither of them is ready. (Ularning hech biri tayyor emas.)

3. "Neither...nor..." strukturasi

Ikkita inkor variantni bog'lash uchun ishlatiladi: "na bu, na u."

Neither ... nor ...

Misol: She likes neither coffee nor tea. (U na kofe, na choyni yaxshi ko'radi.)

Misol: Neither John nor Mary was at home. (Na Jon, na Meri uyda yo'q edi.)

Muhim Qoidalar:

  • "Neither" o'zi inkor ma'nosini beradi, shuning uchun fe'l inkor shaklda bo'lmaydi.
  • "Neither" va "Neither of" dan keyin fe'l odatda birlik shaklda bo'ladi, ayniqsa rasmiy tilda. ("Neither of them is" - to'g'ri)
  • "Neither...nor..." jumlasida fe'l ko'pincha fe'lga eng yaqin bo'lgan otga qarab moslashadi (agar ikkala ot ham birlik yoki ko'plikda bo'lsa).

Either (Ikkalasidan biri / Yoki ... yoki) ‚úÖ

"Either" ikki narsa yoki ikki kishiga nisbatan "ikkalasidan biri" degan ma'noni anglatadi. Bu tanlovni bildiradi.

Ishlatilish Holatlari:

1. Aniqlovchi sifatida: "Either + Singular Noun"

Either + birlikdagi ot

Misol: You can take either road. (Siz ikkala yo'ldan birini tanlashingiz mumkin.)

2. Olmosh sifatida: "Either of + Determiner + Plural Noun / Pronoun (plural object)"

Either of + aniqlovchi + ko'plikdagi ot / ko'plikdagi olmosh

Misol: Either of the answers is acceptable. (Javoblarning ikkalasidan biri qabul qilinadi.)

Misol: You can choose either of them. (Ulardan birini tanlashingiz mumkin.)

3. "Either...or..." strukturasi

Ikkita variantni taklif qilish uchun ishlatiladi: "yoki bu, yoki u."

Either ... or ...

Misol: You can either call me or text me. (Siz menga yoki qo'ng'iroq qilishingiz, yoki xabar yuborishingiz mumkin.)

Misol: Either the dog or the cats are in the garden. (Yoki it, yoki mushuklar bog'da.)

Muhim Qoidalar:

  • "Either" va "Either of" dan keyin fe'l odatda birlik shaklda bo'ladi.
  • "Either...or..." jumlasida fe'l odatda fe'lga eng yaqin bo'lgan otga qarab moslashadi.

Neither vs. Either: Taqqoslash üÜö

Bu ikki so'z bir-biriga o'xshash bo'lsa-da, ularning ma'no va ishlatilishida katta farq bor:

Xususiyat Neither Either
Ma'nosi Ikkalasidan ham hech biri emas (inkor) Ikkalasidan biri (tanlov)
Son Ikkita narsa/odam uchun Ikkita narsa/odam uchun
Fe'l Birlik (odatda), fe'l inkor bo'lmaydi Birlik (odatda)
Bog'lovchi Neither...nor... Either...or...
Misol Neither of them is ready. Either of them is ready.

None (Hech kim / Hech narsa) ‚ûñ

"None" uch yoki undan ortiq odam yoki narsaga nisbatan "hech biri emas" degan ma'noni anglatadi. Bu "all" yoki "any" ning inkoridir.

Ishlatilish Holatlari:

1. "None of + Determiner + Plural Noun / Pronoun (plural object)"

None of + aniqlovchi + ko'plikdagi ot / ko'plikdagi olmosh

Misol: None of my friends smoke. (Do'stlarimning hech biri chekmaydi.)

Misol: None of the apples were ripe. (Olmalarning hech biri pishmagan edi.)

Muhim Qoidalar:

  • "None" uch yoki undan ortiq elementga murojaat qiladi.
  • "None of" dan keyin fe'l birlik yoki ko'plik shaklida bo'lishi mumkin. Birlik shakli ko'proq rasmiy va grammatik jihatdan to'g'ri hisoblanadi. Ko'plik shakli esa norasmiy tilda ko'proq uchraydi.
    • **Birlik fe'l (rasmiy):** None of the students **has** arrived.
    • **Ko'plik fe'l (norasmiy):** None of the students **have** arrived.

Mashqlar üìù

1. To'g'ri So'zni Tanlang (Both, Each, Every, Neither, Either, None)

Gapga mos keladigan to'g'ri so'zni tanlang.

1. _____ of my parents are doctors. (Ikkala ota-onam ham shifokor.)

2. _____ student has to submit their essay by Friday. (Har bir talaba esseini juma kungacha topshirishi kerak.)

3. _____ of the two options is acceptable. (Ikkala variantdan biri ham qabul qilinadi.)

4. _____ of the three solutions worked. (Uchta yechimdan hech biri ishlamadi.)

5. We _____ received a prize. (Har birimiz sovrin oldik.)

2. Bo'sh Joylarni To'ldiring (Tekst Input)

To'g'ri so'zni yozing.

6. Almost _____ person in the room agreed. (Xonadagi deyarli har bir odam rozi bo'ldi.)

7. She likes _____ classical _____ pop music. (U na klassik, na pop musiqasini yaxshi ko'radi.)

8. You can _____ pay by cash _____ by credit card. (Siz naqd pulda yoki kredit karta orqali to'lashingiz mumkin.)

9. _____ of them is correct. (Ikkalasidan hech biri to'g'ri emas.)

10. _____ of the five candidates was suitable. (Besh nomzoddan hech biri mos kelmadi.)

3. Gapni Qayta Tartiblang (Drag & Drop)

So'zlardan to'g'ri gap tuzing.

11. students / has / each / of / the / a / pen.

students has each of the a pen

12. you / or / can / stay / either / leave / .

you or can stay either leave

13. neither / her brother / was / nor / at home / she / .

neither her brother was nor at home she

4. Xatoni Toping va Tuzating

Quyidagi gaplardagi xatolarni toping va to'g'ri javobni yozing.

14. Both of them are not here.

15. Every of the children received a toy.

16. Almost each student passed the test.

17. None of the apples was ripe. (Assume "was" is preferred)
