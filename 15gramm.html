Addition to Remark (Kengaytirilgan)

Fikrlarga Qo'shimcha Ma'lumot Berish San'ati!

Fikrga Qo'shimcha Ma'lumot Berish Nima? 

"Addition to Remark" (fikrga qo'shimcha) - bu suhbat yoki yozish paytida aytilgan fikrga ma'lumot qo'shish, tasdiqlash yoki qarama-qarshi fikr bildirish usuli. Bu takrorlanishdan qochib, gaplarni yanada ravon va tushunarli qilishga yordam beradi.

Ingliz tilida bu uchun "So", "Neither", "Nor", "But" kabi bog'lovchi so'zlar va yordamchi fe'llar (auxiliary verbs) muhim rol o'ynaydi.

1. Ijobiy Fikrga Qo'shimcha (Agreement with Positive Statements) 

Agar siz kimningdir ijobiy fikriga qo'shilmoqchi bo'lsangiz yoki o'zingiz ham xuddi shunday narsani qilyotganingizni aytmoqchi bo'lsangiz, ushbu strukturadan foydalanasiz.

Struktura: So + yordamchi fe'l + ega (subject)

Yordamchi fe'l asosiy gapning zamoniga va egasiga mos bo'lishi kerak.

Asosiy Gap (Positive) Qo'shimcha Fikr Tarjima
She works effectively. So does her team. U samarali ishlaydi. Uning jamoasi ham.
They decided to go ahead. So did I. Ular davom etishga qaror qilishdi. Men ham.
He can speak three languages. So can his brother. U uchta tilda gapira oladi. Uning ukasi ham.
I am ready for the challenge. So am I. Men sinovga tayyorman. Men ham.
We have finished the project. So has John. Biz loyihani tugatdik. Jon ham.
They will arrive soon. So will we. Ular tez orada yetib kelishadi. Biz ham.

Eslatma: "So" so'zi 'ham' ma'nosini beradi. Yordamchi fe'l (do/does, did, can, am, has, will, etc.) asosiy gapdagi fe'lga mos keladi. Egadan oldin kelgani (inversion) ingliz tilidagi xoslikdir.

2. Salbiy Fikrga Qo'shimcha (Agreement with Negative Statements) 

Agar siz kimningdir salbiy fikriga qo'shilmoqchi bo'lsangiz yoki o'zingiz ham xuddi shunday narsani qilmaganingizni aytmoqchi bo'lsangiz, ushbu strukturadan foydalanasiz.

Struktura: Nor / Neither + yordamchi fe'l + ega (subject)

"Nor" va "Neither" bir xil ma'noda ishlatiladi.

Asosiy Gap (Negative) Qo'shimcha Fikr Tarjima
They didn’t face any difficulty. Nor did we. Ular hech qanday qiyinchilikka duch kelishmadi. Biz ham.
She doesn’t work off and on. Neither does he. U vaqtli-vaqtsiz ishlamaydi. U ham.
I have never been to France. Neither has my wife. Men hech qachon Fransiyada bo’lmaganman. Xotinim ham.
He won't come to the party. Nor will I. U bazmga kelmaydi. Men ham.
They are not happy with the results. Neither am I. Ular natijalardan xursand emas. Men ham.
We couldn't solve the problem. Nor could they. Biz muammoni hal qila olmadik. Ular ham.

Eslatma: Bu yerda ham "nor" yoki "neither" dan keyin yordamchi fe'l va keyin ega keladi (inversion). "Never" kabi inkor ma'no beruvchi so'zlar bilan ham "Neither/Nor" ishlatiladi.

3. Fikrlarni Zidlash (Contrast) 

Ba'zan siz bir fikrga qo'shilmasligingiz yoki qarama-qarshi fikr bildirishingiz mumkin. Bunda "but" bog'lovchisi ishlatiladi.

3.1. Ijobiy Gap + Salbiy Fikrga Zidlash

Struktura: Asosiy gap (positive), but + ega + yordamchi fe'l + not

Biror narsa haqida ijobiy gap aytilganda, lekin ikkinchi ega xuddi shu narsani qilmasa.

Asosiy Gap (Positive) Zid Fikr (Negative) Tarjima
He commutes to work, but she does not. U ishga borib keladi, lekin u yo‘q.
They went to the cinema, but we did not. Ular kinoga borishdi, lekin biz bormadik.
I like spicy food, but my brother does not. Men achchiq ovqatni yaxshi ko'raman, lekin ukam yo'q.

3.2. Salbiy Gap + Ijobiy Fikrga Zidlash

Struktura: Asosiy gap (negative), but + ega + yordamchi fe'l

Biror narsa haqida salbiy gap aytilganda, lekin ikkinchi ega xuddi shu narsani qilsa.

Asosiy Gap (Negative) Zid Fikr (Positive) Tarjima
He does not speak effectively, but she does. U samarali gapirmaydi, lekin u gapiradi.
I did not go ahead here, but they did. Men bu yerda davom etmadim, lekin ular etishdi.
They aren't coming, but we are. Ular kelmayapti, lekin biz kelyapmiz.

Eslatma: "But" zid ma'noni bildiradi. Yordamchi fe'l (do/does, did, am/is/are, can, etc.) asosiy gapning zamoniga mos keladi va faqat o'zi, inkor etuvchi "not"siz ishlatiladi (agar ijobiy zidlik bo'lsa) yoki "not" bilan (agar salbiy zidlik bo'lsa).

Asosiy Qoidalar va Eslatmalar 

  • **Yordamchi fe'llar (Auxiliary Verbs):** Har doim asosiy gapning zamoniga va egasiga mos keladigan yordamchi fe'lni ishlating.
    • Present Simple: do/does
    • Past Simple: did
    • Present Continuous: am/is/are
    • Past Continuous: was/were
    • Present Perfect: has/have
    • Modals: can, could, will, would, should, must, might
    • To be fe'li: am/is/are (Present), was/were (Past)
  • **Inversion:** "So", "Neither", "Nor" dan keyin fe'l va ega o'rni almashadi (yordamchi fe'l + ega). "But" bilan esa ega va keyin yordamchi fe'l keladi (normal tartib).
  • **Kontekst:** Qaysi struktura tanlanishi gapning ijobiy yoki salbiyligiga va sizning maqsadingizga (qo'shilish yoki zidlash) bog'liq.

Mashqlar 

Bilimlaringizni sinab ko'ring!

1. Gaplarni To'g'ri Shaklda To'ldiring (Tekst Input)

Bo'sh joylarni mos keladigan "Addition to Remark" shakli bilan to'ldiring. (Misol: "So do I", "Neither is she", "but he does not")

1. She loves classical music. _____ _____ I.

2. My parents aren't coming to the picnic. _____ _____ my brother.

3. He speaks fluent French, _____ _____ _____ I.

4. They have never seen a live concert. _____ _____ we.

5. I didn't forget my keys, _____ _____ he.

2. To'g'ri Variantni Tanlang (Multiple Choice)

Har bir gap uchun to'g'ri variantni tanlang.

6. She won't attend the meeting, (so will her colleague / neither will her colleague).

7. He finished his homework early, (so did I / but I didn't).

8. We aren't going on vacation, (but they are / so are they).

9. My dog loves to play fetch, (nor does my cat / so does my cat).

3. Gapni Qayta Tartiblang (Drag & Drop)

Berilgan so'zlardan to'g'ri gapni tuzing.

10. she / So / is / . / tired

she So is tired

11. did / they / Nor / . / study

did they Nor study

12. but / does / he / not / . / She / speaks / Spanish

but does he not She speaks Spanish

4. Xatoni Toping va Tuzating (Error Correction)

Quyidagi gaplardagi xatolarni toping va to'g'ri javobni yozing.

13. They like rock music, neither do I.

14. He is not good at drawing, but she isn't.

15. She can't cook, so can her brother.

16. We study hard, but they do.
