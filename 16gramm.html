Past Perfect Tense

O'tgan Tugallangan Zamon: O'tmishdagi Harakatlar Ketma-ketligi!

Past Perfect Tense Nima? 

Past Perfect (o'tgan tugallangan) zamoni ingliz tilida o'tmishdagi ikkita harakatdan birining boshqasidan oldin sodir bo'lganini ko'rsatish uchun ishlatiladi. Bu zamon ko'pincha hikoya qilishda yoki o'tmishdagi voqealar ketma-ketligini tushuntirishda juda foydali.

U oddiy qilib aytganda, "o'tmishdagi o'tmish" deb tushunilishi mumkin.

1. Shakli (Form) 

Past Perfect zamoni "had" yordamchi fe'li va asosiy fe'lning uchinchi shakli (Past Participle - V3) yordamida yasaladi.

Struktura: Subject + had + Past Participle (V3)

  • "Had" barcha egalar (I, You, We, They, He, She, It) bilan bir xil ishlatiladi.
Shakl Misol Tarjima
**Affirmative (Darag' gap)** I had finished my work before he arrived. U kelishidan oldin men ishimni tugatgan edim.
She had seen that movie already. U o'sha filmni allaqachon ko'rgan edi.
**Negative (Inkor gap)** We had not (hadn't) eaten anything before the party. Biz ziyofatdan oldin hech narsa yemagan edik.
He had not (hadn't) left when I called. Men qo'ng'iroq qilganimda u ketmagan edi.
**Interrogative (So'roq gap)** Had you ever been to London before 2010? 2010 yildan oldin qachondir Londonda bo'lganmidingiz?
Had she completed the task before the deadline? U vazifani muddatdan oldin tugatganmidi?

Eslatma: Fe'lning uchinchi shakli (Past Participle) odatda to'g'ri fe'llar uchun '-ed' qo'shimchasi bilan yasaladi (walk - walked - walked), noto'g'ri fe'llar esa maxsus shaklga ega (go - went - gone, eat - ate - eaten).

2. Ishlatilish Holatlari (Uses) 

Past Perfect zamoni asosan bitta asosiy holatda ishlatiladi:

2.1. O'tmishdagi Boshqa Bir Harakatdan Oldin Sodir Bo'lgan Harakat

Bu Past Perfect zamonining eng keng tarqalgan ishlatilishi. O'tmishda sodir bo'lgan ikkita harakat bo'lganda, avval sodir bo'lganini Past Perfectda, keyin sodir bo'lganini esa Past Simpleda ifodalaymiz.

Misol: When I arrived at the station, the train had already left. (Men stansiyaga yetib kelganimda, poezd allaqachon ketib bo'lgan edi.)
*Poezdning ketishi (Past Perfect) men yetib kelishimdan (Past Simple) oldin sodir bo'lgan.*

Misol: She had studied English before she moved to London. (U Londonga ko'chib o'tishdan oldin ingliz tilini o'rgangan edi.)
*Ingliz tilini o'rganishi (Past Perfect) Londonga ko'chib o'tishidan (Past Simple) oldin sodir bo'lgan.*

Misol: He recognized me because he had seen me before. (U meni tanidi, chunki u meni ilgari ko'rgan edi.)
*Uning meni ko'rishi (Past Perfect) meni tanishidan (Past Simple) oldin sodir bo'lgan.*

2.2. O'tmishdagi Ma'lum Bir Nuqtagacha Davom Etgan Harakat (Vaqt Ko'rsatkichlari bilan)

O'tmishdagi biror aniq nuqtagacha davom etgan harakatlar yoki holatlar. Ko'pincha "for" va "since" bilan ishlatiladi.

Misol: I had lived in that city for five years before I moved here. (Bu yerga ko'chib kelishimdan oldin o'sha shaharda besh yil yashagan edim.)

Misol: They had been married for 20 years when he died. (U o'lganida, ular 20 yildan buyon turmush qurgan edilar.)

3. Vaqt Ko'rsatkichlari (Time Expressions) 

Past Perfect zamoni bilan tez-tez ishlatiladigan vaqt ko'rsatkichlari:

  • **Before:** Oldin sodir bo'lgan harakatni Past Simple bilan bog'lash uchun.
  • **After:** Keyin sodir bo'lgan harakatni Past Simple bilan bog'lash uchun.
  • **When:** Ikki harakat orasidagi bog'liqlikni ko'rsatish uchun.
  • **By the time:** Biror vaqt yoki hodisa sodir bo'lguncha. (e.g., *By the time I woke up, she had already left.*)
  • **Already:** Biror harakat boshqasidan oldin tugaganini ta'kidlash uchun.
  • **Just:** Biror harakat boshqasidan biroz oldin tugaganini ko'rsatish uchun.
  • **For:** O'tmishdagi biror nuqtagacha davom etgan vaqt davomiyligi.
  • **Since:** O'tmishdagi biror nuqtagacha boshlangan va davom etgan vaqt boshlanishi.

4. Past Perfect vs. Past Simple 

Bu ikki zamonni to'g'ri ishlatish uchun ularning farqini tushunish juda muhim:

  • **Past Simple:** O'tmishda boshlangan va o'tmishda tugallangan, aniq vaqti ma'lum bo'lgan bir harakatni ifodalaydi. (e.g., *I ate dinner yesterday.*)
  • **Past Perfect:** O'tmishda sodir bo'lgan BOSHQA BIR harakatdan OLDIN sodir bo'lgan harakatni ifodalaydi. Bu yerda ikkita o'tmishdagi harakat bor va ularning ketma-ketligi muhim.
Past Perfect Past Simple
When I arrived, she had already left. (U men kelishimdan oldin ketgan) When I arrived, she left. (Men kelganimda u ketdi - ikkita harakat deyarli bir vaqtda sodir bo'lgan)
I had finished my book by 9 PM. (Kitobni 9 gacha tugatib bo'lgan edim) I finished my book at 9 PM. (Kitobni 9 da tugatdim)

Eslatma: Past Perfect ko'pincha Past Simple bilan birga keladi. Agar gapda faqat bitta o'tmishdagi harakat bo'lsa va uning aniq vaqti ko'rsatilgan bo'lsa, odatda Past Simple ishlatiladi.

Mashqlar 

1. Bo'sh Joylarni To'ldiring (Tekst Input)

Fe'llarni Past Perfect zamonida yozing.

1. By the time I woke up, the sun _____ already _____ (rise).

2. She _____ (not / hear) the news before I told her.

3. After they _____ (eat) dinner, they went for a walk.

4. He realized he _____ (forget) his wallet at home.

5. _____ you ever _____ (visit) that city before you moved there?

2. To'g'ri Variantni Tanlang (Multiple Choice)

Past Perfect yoki Past Simple'dan to'g'risini tanlang.

6. When I arrived, the meeting (had already started / already started).

7. She (had lived / lived) in Paris for five years before she moved to Rome.

8. We (had finished / finished) our work, then we went home.

9. They realized they (had made / made) a mistake.

3. Gapni Qayta Tartiblang (Drag & Drop)

So'zlardan to'g'ri Past Perfect gapini tuzing.

10. had / the / movie / ended / already / when / arrived / we / .

had the movie ended already when arrived we

11. because / he / seen / him / had / before / He / recognized / him / .

because he seen him had before He recognized

4. Xatoni Toping va Tuzating (Error Correction)

Quyidagi gaplardagi xatolarni toping va to'g'ri javobni yozing.

12. When I came home, my brother already ate dinner.

13. She studied English for three years before she moves to Canada.

14. I didn't see him because he went earlier.
