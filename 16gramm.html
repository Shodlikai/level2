Present Perfect Tense

Hozirgi Tugallangan Zamon: Qachon va Qanday Ishlatiladi?

Present Perfect Tense Nima? ü§î

Present Perfect zamoni ingliz tilidagi eng ko'p chalkashtiriladigan, ammo eng muhim zamonlardan biridir. U o'tmishda boshlangan, ammo hozirgi zamon bilan qandaydir aloqasi bor bo'lgan harakatlar yoki holatlarni ifodalash uchun ishlatiladi. Bu zamon ko'pincha tugallangan harakatlarning hozirgi natijasini ta'kidlaydi.

1. Shakli (Form) üìù

Present Perfect zamoni "have/has" yordamchi fe'li va asosiy fe'lning uchinchi shakli (Past Participle - V3) yordamida yasaladi.

Struktura: Subject + have/has + Past Participle (V3)

  • **I, You, We, They** bilan **have** ishlatiladi.
  • **He, She, It** bilan **has** ishlatiladi.
Shakl Misol Tarjima
**Affirmative (Darag' gap)** I have finished my homework. Men uy vazifamni tugatdim.
She has seen that movie. U o'sha filmni ko'rgan.
**Negative (Inkor gap)** We have not (haven't) eaten yet. Biz hali ovqatlanmadik.
He has not (hasn't) arrived. U yetib kelmadi.
**Interrogative (So'roq gap)** Have you ever been to London? Siz qachondir Londonda bo'lganmisiz?
Has she completed the task? U vazifani tugatdimi?

Eslatma: Fe'lning uchinchi shakli (Past Participle) odatda to'g'ri fe'llar uchun '-ed' qo'shimchasi bilan yasaladi (walk - walked - walked), noto'g'ri fe'llar esa maxsus shaklga ega (go - went - gone, eat - ate - eaten).

2. Ishlatilish Holatlari (Uses) üéØ

Present Perfect zamoni bir nechta asosiy holatlarda ishlatiladi:

2.1. Natijasi Hozirgi Zamonga Tegishli Tugallangan Harakatlar

O'tmishda sodir bo'lgan va yakunlangan, lekin uning natijasi hozirgi zamonda muhim bo'lgan harakatlar.

Misol: I have lost my keys. (Kalitlarimni yo'qotdim. Natijasi: Hozir ularni topa olmayapman.)

Misol: She has cleaned her room. (U o'z xonasini tozaladi. Natijasi: Hozir xonasi toza.)

2.2. Hozirgacha Tajriba (Experience Up To Now)

Hayot davomida hozirgacha sodir bo'lgan tajribalar haqida gapirish uchun. Ko'pincha "ever" (qachondir), "never" (hech qachon), "before" (ilgari), "many times" (ko'p marta) kabi so'zlar bilan ishlatiladi.

Misol: Have you ever visited Japan? (Siz qachondir Yaponiyaga borganmisiz?)

Misol: I have never eaten sushi before. (Men ilgari hech qachon sushini yemaganman.)

Misol: We have seen that movie three times. (Biz bu filmni uch marta ko'rganmiz.)

2.3. Hozirgi Paytgacha Davom Etayotgan Harakatlar yoki Holatlar

O'tmishda boshlangan va hozirgacha davom etayotgan harakatlar yoki holatlar. Ko'pincha "for" (davomida) va "since" (dan beri) bilan ishlatiladi.

Misol: I have lived in Tashkent for 10 years. (Men Toshkentda 10 yildan buyon yashayapman.)

Misol: She has worked here since 2020. (U bu yerda 2020 yildan beri ishlayapti.)

Misol: They haven't seen each other since last year. (Ular o'tgan yildan beri bir-birlarini ko'rmaganlar.)

2.4. Yaqinda Tugallangan Harakatlar

Juda yaqinda tugallangan va uning natijasi hozirgi paytda ko'rinib turgan harakatlar. Ko'pincha "just" (hozirgina), "already" (allaqachon), "yet" (hali) kabi so'zlar bilan ishlatiladi.

Misol: He has just left. (U hozirgina ketdi.)

Misol: We have already finished dinner. (Biz allaqachon kechki ovqatni tugatdik.)

Misol: Has she arrived yet? (U hali kelmadimi? / U allaqachon keldimi?)

Misol: No, she hasn't arrived yet. (Yo'q, u hali kelmadi.)

3. Vaqt Ko'rsatkichlari (Time Expressions) ‚è±Ô∏è

Present Perfect zamoni bilan tez-tez ishlatiladigan vaqt ko'rsatkichlari:

  • **For:** Ma'lum bir vaqt davomiyligini (kunlar, oylar, yillar) bildiradi. (e.g., *for two hours, for a long time*)
  • **Since:** Ma'lum bir boshlang'ich nuqtani bildiradi. (e.g., *since Monday, since 2005, since I was a child*)
  • **Just:** Juda yaqinda tugallangan harakat.
  • **Already:** Harakat kutilganidan tezroq tugallangan.
  • **Yet:** Inkor va so'roq gaplarda ishlatiladi. 'Hali' (inkorda) yoki 'allaqachon' (so'roqda) ma'nosida. Gapning oxirida keladi.
  • **Ever:** Hayotdagi tajribalar haqidagi so'roq gaplarda. ('qachondir' ma'nosida)
  • **Never:** Hayotdagi tajribalar haqidagi inkor gaplarda. ('hech qachon' ma'nosida)
  • **Recently / Lately:** Yaqinda sodir bo'lgan harakatlar.
  • **So far / Up to now:** Hozirgacha erishilgan natijalar.

4. Present Perfect vs. Past Simple üÜö

Bu ikki zamonni farqlash juda muhim:

  • **Past Simple:** Harakat o'tmishda boshlangan va o'tmishda tugallangan. Uning hozirgi zamon bilan hech qanday aloqasi yo'q va aniq o'tmish vaqti ko'rsatiladi. (e.g., *yesterday, last week, in 2005*)
  • **Present Perfect:** Harakat o'tmishda boshlangan, ammo hozirgi zamon bilan aloqasi bor (natijasi, tajribasi, davomiyligi). Aniq o'tmish vaqti ko'rsatilmaydi.
Present Perfect Past Simple
I have visited Paris. (Hayotimda qachondir, aniq vaqti muhim emas, balki tajriba) I visited Paris last year. (Aniq o'tgan yil - vaqti ma'lum)
She has lost her phone. (Hozir telefoni yo'q) She lost her phone yesterday. (Kecha yo'qotgan, endi muhim emasmi yo topib olganmi - muhimi o'tmishda tugagan vaqt)

Eslatma: Agar aniq o'tmish vaqti (e.g., yesterday, last month, in 1999) berilgan bo'lsa, har doim Past Simple ishlatiladi.

Mashqlar üìù

1. Bo'sh Joylarni To'ldiring (Tekst Input)

Fe'llarni Present Perfect zamonida yozing.

1. I _____ (finish) my work.

2. She _____ (not / see) that movie yet.

3. _____ you ever _____ (eat) sushi?

4. They _____ (live) here since 2010.

5. He _____ just _____ (arrive).

2. To'g'ri Variantni Tanlang (Multiple Choice)

Present Perfect yoki Past Simple'dan to'g'risini tanlang.

6. I (have visited / visited) Rome last year.

7. She (has been / was) to Japan twice in her life.

8. We (haven't seen / didn't see) him since Monday.

9. They (have bought / bought) a new car yesterday.

3. Gapni Qayta Tartiblang (Drag & Drop)

So'zlardan to'g'ri Present Perfect gapini tuzing.

10. you / Have / ever / a / ridden / horse / ?

you Have ever a ridden horse ?

11. not / has / yet / arrived / He / .

not has yet arrived He

4. Xatoni Toping va Tuzating (Error Correction)

Quyidagi gaplardagi xatolarni toping va to'g'ri javobni yozing.

12. I am living here for five years.

13. Did you ever see a ghost?

14. She already finished her homework.
