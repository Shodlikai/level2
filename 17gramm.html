Past Perfect Continuous (Tugallangan Davomli O'tgan Zamon)

O'tgan Zamondagi Davomli Harakatlarni Ifodalash San'ati!

Past Perfect Continuous Nima? ‚è≥

"Past Perfect Continuous" (tugallangan davomli o'tgan zamon) o'tgan zamonda ma'lum bir nuqtadan oldin boshlangan va o'sha nuqtaga qadar davom etgan harakat yoki holatni ifodalash uchun ishlatiladi. Bu zamon ko'pincha boshqa bir o'tgan zamondagi hodisa yoki vaqt ko'rsatkichi bilan birga qo'llaniladi.

Bu zamon yordamida biz o'tgan zamondagi voqeaning davomiyligini ta'kidlaymiz va uni boshqa bir o'tgan zamondagi hodisa bilan bog'laymiz.

1. Past Perfect Continuous Strukturalari üìö

Past Perfect Continuous zamonining asosiy strukturasi quyidagicha:

1.1. Darax gap (Positive Statements)

Struktura: Subject + had been + V-ing (fe'lning tuslangan shakli) + Object/Complement

Bu struktura o'tgan zamonda ma'lum bir nuqtaga qadar davom etgan harakatni ifodalaydi.

Asosiy Gap Tarjima
She **had been working** for hours before she took a break. U tanaffus qilishidan oldin bir necha soat ishlagan edi.
They **had been waiting** for the bus since morning when it finally arrived. Ular avtobus kelgunicha ertalabdan beri kutishayotgan edi.
He **had been living** in London for five years before he moved to Paris. U Parijga ko'chib o'tishidan oldin besh yil Londonda yashayotgan edi.
I **had been studying** English for three years by the time I moved to the UK. Men Buyuk Britaniyaga ko'chib o'tganimda uch yildan beri ingliz tili o'rganayotgan edim.
We **had been traveling** all day when we reached the destination. Biz manzilga yetib kelganimizda butun kun sayohat qilayotgan edik.

1.2. Inkor gap (Negative Statements)

Struktura: Subject + had not been + V-ing (fe'lning tuslangan shakli) + Object/Complement

Qisqartirilgan shakli: **hadn't been**.

Asosiy Gap Tarjima
She **hadn't been feeling** well for days before she saw a doctor. U shifokorga ko'rinishidan oldin bir necha kundan beri o'zini yaxshi his qilmayotgan edi.
They **had not been preparing** for the exam properly, so they failed. Ular imtihonga to'g'ri tayyorgarlik ko'rmagan edilar, shuning uchun yiqilishdi.
I **hadn't been sleeping** enough, which made me tired. Men yetarli uxlamayotgan edim, bu meni charchatdi.
He **had not been paying** attention during the lecture, so he missed the main point. U ma'ruza davomida diqqat qilmayotgan edi, shuning uchun asosiy nuqtani o'tkazib yubordi.

1.3. So'roq gap (Interrogative Statements)

Struktura: Had + Subject + been + V-ing (fe'lning tuslangan shakli) + Object/Complement?

Ha/Yo'q javob talab qiluvchi savollar uchun.

Asosiy Gap Tarjima
**Had** she **been crying** before you arrived? Siz kelishingizdan oldin u yig'layotganmidi?
**Had** they **been discussing** the plan all morning? Ular butun ertalab rejani muhokama qilishayotganmidi?
**Had** you **been waiting** long when the manager called you? Menejer sizni chaqirganida, uzoq kutayotganmidingiz?

1.4. Maxsus so'roq gaplar (WH-questions)

Struktura: WH-word + had + Subject + been + V-ing (fe'lning tuslangan shakli) + Object/Complement?

Asosiy Gap Tarjima
**What had they been doing** before the electricity went out? Elektr uzilishidan oldin ular nima qilayotgan edilar?
**Why had she been avoiding** him for so long? Nima uchun u uni shuncha vaqtdan beri chetlab o'tayotgan edi?
**How long had you been living** there when the earthquake happened? Zilzila bo'lganida siz u yerda qancha vaqtdan beri yashayotgan edingiz?

Asosiy Qoidalar va Eslatmalar üìù

  • **Qo'llanilishi:** Past Perfect Continuous, o'tgan zamondagi ma'lum bir nuqtagacha davom etgan, shu nuqtada tugallangan yoki shu nuqtaga qadar ta'sirini ko'rsatgan harakatlarni tasvirlash uchun ishlatiladi.
  • **Kalit so'zlar (Time Expressions):** Bu zamon bilan ko'pincha quyidagi vaqt ko'rsatkichlari ishlatiladi:
    • **for** (davomiylikni bildiradi: _for two hours, for a long time_)
    • **since** (boshlanish nuqtasini bildiradi: _since morning, since 2010_)
    • **all day/night/week/month/year** (butun davomida)
    • **by the time** (biror vaqtga qadar)
    • **before** (oldin)
    • **until** (qadar)
  • **Boshqa zamonlar bilan bog'liqlik:** Ko'pincha Past Simple bilan birga ishlatiladi, chunki Past Simple o'tgan zamondagi boshqa bir harakat yoki nuqtani ko'rsatadi.
    • Misol: _I **had been waiting** for an hour when he **arrived**._ (U kelganida bir soatdan beri kutayotgan edim.)
  • **Holat fe'llari (Stative Verbs):** Holat fe'llari (masalan, _know, believe, understand, like, hate, own, seem_) odatda davomli zamonlarda ishlatilmaydi. Ular o'rniga Past Perfect ishlatiladi.
    • Xato: _I had been knowing him for years._
    • To'g'ri: _I **had known** him for years._

1. Gaplarni To'g'ri Shaklda To'ldiring (Tekst Input)

Bo'sh joylarni Past Perfect Continuous zamonida mos keladigan fe'l shakli bilan to'ldiring. (Misol: "had been waiting")

1. She (study) _____ _____ _____ for three hours when her phone rang.

2. They (play) _____ _____ _____ football all afternoon before it started raining.

3. I (live) _____ _____ _____ in that city for ten years before I moved here.

4. He (not / sleep) _____ _____ _____ well for days, so he was exhausted.

5. How long (you / work) _____ _____ _____ _____ on this project when it was completed?

2. To'g'ri Variantni Tanlang (Multiple Choice)

Har bir gap uchun to'g'ri variantni tanlang.

6. She was tired because she (had been running / had run) a marathon.

7. By the time I arrived, they (had been discussing / had discussed) the issue for an hour.

8. We (had been waiting / had waited) for him since noon when he finally showed up.

9. How long (had she been teaching / had she taught) before she retired?

3. Gapni Qayta Tartiblang (Drag & Drop)

Berilgan so'zlardan to'g'ri gapni tuzing.

10. working / had / she / all / been / day / .

working had she all been day

11. had / they / not / sleeping / been / well / .

had they not sleeping been well

12. you / been / where / had / going / ?

you been where had going

4. Xatoni Toping va Tuzating (Error Correction)

Quyidagi gaplardagi xatolarni toping va to'g'ri javobni yozing.

13. She had been know him for years before they got married.

14. They had been waited for an hour when the flight was cancelled.

15. How long had you been lived in Tashkent when you met her?

16. He was tired because he had been ran all morning.
