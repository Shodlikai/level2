Ingliz tili - Pronouns Darsi

Reflexive, Reciprocal va Indefinite Pronouns

Reflexive Pronouns (Qaytim olmoshlari)

Reflexive pronouns - bu harakat qiluvchi shaxsning o'zi ustiga qaytgan harakatni ifodalaydi

Nima uchun ishlatiladi?

1. O'zini anglash

Harakat qiluvchi o'zi ustiga harakat qilganda

I cut myself - Men o'zimni kesdim

2. Mustaqil harakat

Biror ishni o'zi qilganda

He did it himself - U buni o'zi qildi

Reflexive Pronouns jadvali

Shaxs Reflexive Pronoun Misol
I myself I believe in myself
You yourself You should trust yourself
He himself He taught himself English
She herself She looked at herself
It itself The cat cleaned itself
We ourselves We enjoyed ourselves
You (ko'plik) yourselves You should help yourselves
They themselves They prepared themselves

Qo'llanish qoidalari

1. Ob'ekt sifatida

Gapning ob'ekti bo'lib kelganda

✓ I taught myself to cook
✗ I taught me to cook

2. Predlog bilan

Predloglardan keyin ishlatiladi

✓ She bought a gift for herself
✗ She bought a gift for her

Mashq 1: To'g'ri javobni tanlang

I need to take care of _____ health.

The children enjoyed _____ at the party.

Reciprocal Pronouns (O'zaro olmoshlar)

Reciprocal pronouns - ikki yoki ko'p shaxs o'rtasidagi o'zaro harakatni ifodalaydi

each other

Odatda ikki shaxs o'rtasida ishlatiladi

Tom and Mary love each other
Tom va Mary bir-birlarini yaxshi ko'radilar

one another

Uch yoki ko'p shaxs o'rtasida ishlatiladi

The team members help one another
Jamoa a'zolari bir-birlariga yordam berishadi

Each other vs One another farqi

Each other One another
2 shaxs uchun 3+ shaxs uchun
They kissed each other All students helped one another
The couple talked to each other The family members support one another

Mashq 2: Each other yoki One another?

The husband and wife respect _____.

All the students in the class help _____.

Indefinite Pronouns (Noaniq olmoshlar)

Indefinite pronouns - aniq bo'lmagan shaxs, narsa yoki miqdorni ifodalaydi

Shaxslar uchun

someone/somebody

kimdir

Someone called you
anyone/anybody

har kim, hech kim

Anyone can do this
everyone/everybody

hamma

Everyone is here
no one/nobody

hech kim

Nobody knows

Narsalar uchun

something

nimadir

I need something
anything

hamma narsa, hech narsa

I don't want anything
everything

hamma narsa

Everything is ready
nothing

hech narsa

Nothing happened

Joylar uchun

somewhere

qayerdir

Let's go somewhere
anywhere

har qayerda, hech qayerda

I can't find it anywhere
everywhere

hamma yerda

I looked everywhere
nowhere

hech qayerda

There's nowhere to go

Qo'llanish qoidalari

1. Tasdiqlash gaplarida

Some- bilan boshlanadigan so'zlar ishlatiladi

✓ Someone is at the door
✓ I want something to eat

2. Inkor va savol gaplarida

Any- bilan boshlanadigan so'zlar ishlatiladi

✓ I don't know anyone
✓ Did you see anything?

3. Birlik shaklida

Barcha indefinite pronouns birlik shaklida ishlatiladi

✓ Everyone is ready
✗ Everyone are ready

Mashq 3: To'g'ri indefinite pronoun tanlang

I don't know _____ about this topic.

_____ wants to come to the party.
