Future Perfect Tense: Kelasi Tugallangan Zamon

Kelajakda biror ish tugallangan bo'lishini ifodalash!

Kelasi Tugallangan Zamon Nima?

Kelasi tugallangan zamon (Future Perfect Tense) kelajakdagi ma'lum bir nuqtaga qadar yakunlanadigan harakatlarni ifodalash uchun ishlatiladi. Bu zamon, asosan, kelajakda biror ish tugallangan bo'lishini taxmin qilish yoki belgilashda qo'llaniladi. Ko'pincha bu zamonni aniq bir kelasi vaqtni bildiruvchi "by" (gacha, -ga qadar) yoki "before" (avval) kabi predloglar bilan ishlatamiz.

Future Perfect Tense asosan "will have + fe'lning III shakli (past participle)" strukturasi yordamida hosil bo'ladi.

1. Ijobiy Shakl (Positive Form)

Ega (Subject) + will have + Fe'lning III shakli (Past Participle)

Misol: I will have finished my homework by 8 PM. (Men soat 8 gacha uy vazifamni tugatgan bo'laman.)

Misol: She will have arrived by tomorrow morning. (U ertaga tonggacha yetib kelgan bo'ladi.)

2. Inkor Shakl (Negative Form)

Ega (Subject) + will not (won't) have + Fe'lning III shakli (Past Participle)

Misol: I will not have finished my homework by 8 PM. (Men soat 8 gacha uy vazifamni tugatmagan bo'laman.)

Misol: They won't have built the new bridge by next year. (Ular kelasi yilgacha yangi ko'prikni qurib bo'lishmaydi.)

3. So'roq Shakli (Interrogative Form)

Will + Ega (Subject) + have + Fe'lning III shakli (Past Participle)?

Misol: Will you have finished your homework by 8 PM? (Siz soat 8 gacha uy vazifangizni tugatgan bo'lasizmi?)

Misol: Will she have arrived by tomorrow morning? (U ertaga tonggacha yetib kelgan bo'ladimi?)

Future Perfect Tense Ishlatilish Holatlari üí°

1. Kelajakda Ma'lum Bir Nuqtaga Qadar Tugallanadigan Harakatlar

Bu, Future Perfect Tense'ning asosiy ishlatilishidir. Harakat kelajakda boshlanadi va kelajakdagi boshqa bir harakatdan oldin yoki kelajakdagi aniq bir vaqtga qadar tugaydi.

Misol: By next month, I will have lived in London for five years. (Keyingi oyga qadar, men Londonda besh yil yashagan bo'laman.)

Misol: By the time you arrive, we will have already eaten dinner. (Siz yetib kelguningizcha, biz allaqachon kechki ovqatni yeb bo'lamiz.)

2. Kelajakda Biror Harakatning Sababini Yoki Natijasini Ko'rsatish

Bu zamon kelajakdagi boshqa bir harakat yoki vaziyatning sababini yoki natijasini ko'rsatish uchun ham ishlatilishi mumkin.

Misol: He will be tired because he will have worked all night. (U charchagan bo'ladi, chunki u butun tun ishlagan bo'ladi.)

3. Kelajakdagi Taxminlar (Predictions)

Kelajakda biror narsa allaqachon sodir bo'lgan bo'lishi haqida taxmin qilishda.

Misol: They will have heard the news by now. (Ular hozirgacha yangilikni eshitgan bo'lishadi.)

Future Perfect Tense Kalit So'zlari üîë

Future Perfect Tense, asosan, kelajakdagi aniq bir nuqtaga qadar biror harakatning tugallangan bo'lishini ifodalaganligi sababli, uning kalit so'zlari ham shu vaqt chegarasini belgilaydi.

1. By + kelasi vaqt/sana

Bu eng keng tarqalgan kalit so'z bo'lib, "‚Ä¶gacha", "‚Ä¶ga qadar" degan ma'noni beradi.

  • By tomorrow (ertagacha)
  • By next week/month/year (keyingi hafta/oy/yilgacha)
  • By 5 o'clock (soat 5 gacha)
  • By the end of the day/week/month (kun/hafta/oy oxirigacha)
  • By 2030 (2030 yilgacha)

Misol: She will have finished her report by tomorrow morning. (U ertaga tonggacha hisobotini tugatgan bo'ladi.)

2. By then

"O'shanda gacha" yoki "o'sha vaqtga qadar" degan ma'noni beradi va avvalroq tilga olingan vaqtga ishora qiladi.

Misol: We'll be very busy this week. By then, we will have completed three major projects. (Bu hafta juda band bo'lamiz. O'shanda gacha, biz uchta yirik loyihani yakunlagan bo'lamiz.)

3. By the time + gap (Present Simpleda)

"...ga qadar", "...bo'lguniga qadar" degan ma'noni anglatadi. "By the time" dan keyin odatda Present Simple Tense ishlatiladi, chunki u kelajakdagi bir nuqtani belgilaydi.

  • By the time you arrive (siz yetib kelguningizcha)
  • By the time the sun sets (quyosh botguniga qadar)

Misol: By the time you wake up, I will have already left for work. (Siz uyg'onguningizcha, men allaqachon ishga ketgan bo'laman.)

4. Before + kelasi vaqt/gap

"...avval", "‚Ä¶dan oldin" degan ma'noni beradi.

  • Before Saturday (shanbagacha)
  • Before the meeting starts (yig'ilish boshlanishidan avval)

Misol: They will have completed the training before the new system is implemented. (Ular yangi tizim joriy etilishidan avval treningni yakunlagan bo'lishadi.)

5. In + vaqt oralig'i (kelajakda)

"‚Ä¶dan keyin" ma'nosida ishlatilganda ham Future Perfect bilan kelishi mumkin, ammo bu kamroq uchraydi va asosan ma'lum bir tugash nuqtasiga urg'u beradi.

  • In two years (ikki yildan keyin)

Misol: In three months, she will have learned enough Spanish to travel. (Uch oy ichida u sayohat qilish uchun yetarli ispan tilini o'rgangan bo'ladi.)

Mashqlar üìù

1. Gaplarni Future Perfect Tense'da Qayta Yozing

Qavs ichidagi fe'llarni Future Perfect Tense'ga qo'ying.

1. By next year, they (build) a new house.

2. She (not / complete) the project by Friday.

3. (You / read) this book by the time I return?

4. By 2030, humans (travel) to Mars.

5. We (finish) dinner before the movie starts.

2. To'g'ri Shaklni Tanlang

Quyidagi gaplarda to'g'ri fe'l shaklini tanlang.

6. By the end of the month, I (will save / will have saved) enough money for a new car.

7. When you call me tomorrow, I (will leave / will have left) the office.

8. He (won't repair / won't have repaired) the car by the weekend.

9. (Will they start / Will they have started) the meeting by 9 AM?

10. By that time, she (will learn / will have learned) English perfectly.

3. Gaplarni Tuzing (Drag & Drop)

So'zlardan Future Perfect Tense'da to'g'ri gap tuzing.

11. project / they / by / have / will / completed / the / deadline.

project they by have will completed the deadline

12. before / finished / I / the / will / presentation / have / lunch.

before finished I the will presentation have lunch

13. not / you / by / have / forgotten / will / me / then?

not you by have forgotten will me then?

4. Xatoni Toping va Tuzating

Quyidagi gaplardagi xatolarni toping va to'g'ri javobni yozing.

14. By next year, I will finished my studies.

15. She will has started a new job by September.

16. Will they have prepare dinner when we get there?

17. We won't finished this task until tomorrow.
