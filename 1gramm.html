Past Simple Qoidalari

1. Past Simple nima?

Past Simple - o'tgan zamonda sodir bo'lgan va tugagan ishlarni ifodalash uchun ishlatiladi.

2. Qachon ishlatiladi?

  • O'tmishda aniq vaqtda sodir bo'lgan ishlar uchun
  • O'tmishda takrorlanuvchi ishlar uchun
  • O'tmishda ketma-ket sodir bo'lgan ishlar uchun

3. Vaqt ko'rsatkichlari

yesterday last week ago in 2020 when

Past Simple Strukturalari

Darak gap (+)

Subject + V2 (Past form) + Object

Misol: I played football yesterday.

Tarjima: Men kecha futbol o'ynadim.

Inkor gap (-)

Subject + did not (didn't) + V1 (Base form) + Object

Misol: I didn't play football yesterday.

Tarjima: Men kecha futbol o'ynamadim.

So'roq gap (?)

Did + Subject + V1 (Base form) + Object + ?

Misol: Did you play football yesterday?

Tarjima: Sen kecha futbol o'ynadingmi?

Misollar

Darak gaplar

I watched a movie last night.

Men kecha kechqurun film tomosha qildim.

She visited her grandmother yesterday.

U kecha buvijiga tashrif buyurdi.

We studied English two hours ago.

Biz ikki soat avval ingliz tilini o'rgandik.

They played basketball in the park.

Ular parkda basketbol o'ynashdi.

He worked at the office yesterday.

U kecha ofisda ishladi.

Inkor gaplar

I didn't watch TV yesterday.

Men kecha televizor tomosha qilmadim.

She didn't come to the party.

U ziyofatga kelmadi.

We didn't finish our homework.

Biz uy vazifamizni tugatmadik.

They didn't play football today.

Ular bugun futbol o'ynamadilar.

He didn't call me last week.

U o'tgan hafta menga qo'ng'iroq qilmadi.

So'roq gaplar

Did you eat breakfast this morning?

Sen bugun ertalab nonushta qildingmi?

Did she buy a new car?

U yangi mashina sotib oldimi?

Did they go to the cinema?

Ular kinoteatrga borishdimi?

Did he finish his work?

U o'z ishini tugatdimi?

Did we meet before?

Biz avval uchrashdikmi?
