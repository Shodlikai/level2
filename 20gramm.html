 Prepositions of Movement

Asosiy Tushuncha

Prepositions of Movement (Harakat predloglari) harakat yo'nalishi, manzil va harakatning qayerdan qayerga bo'lishini ko'rsatadi.

Asosiy Predloglar

TO • INTO • ONTO • THROUGH • ACROSS • ALONG • UP • DOWN
TO: I'm going to school. (Maktabga boraman)
INTO: He walked into the room. (U xonaga kirdi)
THROUGH: We drove through the tunnel. (Tunnel orqali haydadik)

Misollar

TO: She is walking to the park.
UZ: U bog'ga yurmoqda.
INTO: The cat jumped into the box.
UZ: Mushuk qutiga sakrab kirdi.
ONTO: He climbed onto the roof.
UZ: U tomga chiqdi.
THROUGH: We walked through the forest.
UZ: Biz o'rmon orqali yurdik.
ACROSS: They swam across the river.
UZ: Ular daryoni suzib o'tdi.
ALONG: We drove along the coast.
UZ: Biz qirg'oq bo'ylab haydadik.

Asosiy Predloglar

TO

Manzil, yo'nalish

go to, come to, walk to
INTO

Ichiga kirish

go into, jump into, fall into
ONTO

Ustiga chiqish

climb onto, jump onto
THROUGH

Orqali o'tish

go through, walk through
ACROSS

Bir tarafdan ikkinchisiga

go across, swim across
ALONG

Bo'ylab, yo'l bo'yicha

walk along, drive along
UP/DOWN

Yuqoriga/pastga

go up, come down
AROUND

Atrofida, aylanib

walk around, go around

Asosiy Qoidalar

1. TO vs INTO farqi

TO - umumiy yo'nalish, INTO - ichiga kirish harakati

2. ACROSS vs THROUGH farqi

ACROSS - yuza bo'ylab, THROUGH - ichki qismidan o'tish

3. ONTO vs ON farqi

ONTO - ustiga chiqish harakati, ON - holatni ko'rsatadi

4. Fe'l bilan birga

Harakat predloglari doimo harakat fe'llari bilan ishlatiladi

Xulosa

Prepositions of Movement to'g'ri ishlatish uchun:

  • Har bir predlogning ma'nosini aniq bilib oling
  • TO va INTO, ACROSS va THROUGH farqlarini eslang
  • Harakat fe'llari bilan birgalikda ishlating
  • Tasviriy gaplar bilan ko'proq mashq qiling
  • Vizual tasavvur qiling - bu juda yordam beradi!
