Preposition of Movement nima?

Harakat predloglari - bu harakat yo'nalishi, boshlang'ich nuqta va tugash nuqtasini ko'rsatadigan so'zlardir.

FROM

INTO

OUT OF

Jump ONTO the table

Gaplar Strukturasi

Darak Gap (Affirmative)

Subject + Verb + Preposition + Object
She walks to school
They drive through the tunnel

Inkor Gap (Negative)

Subject + do/does + not + Verb + Preposition + Object
He doesn't go into the room
We don't walk across the bridge

So'roq Gap (Interrogative)

Do/Does + Subject + Verb + Preposition + Object?
Does she run around the park?
Do you come from home?

Wh-Questions

Wh-word + do/does + Subject + Verb + Preposition?
Where does he go to?
How do they get through?

Transport

Sport

He jumps onto the platform. U platformaga sakraydi.
She runs around the track. U trek atrofida yuguradi.
They climb up the mountain. Ular tog'ga chiqishadi.
