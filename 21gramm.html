üéØAsosiy Tushuncha

Future Perfect Continuous zamoni kelajakda ma'lum bir paytga kelib, harakatning qancha vaqt davom etganini ko'rsatadi.

üîßAsosiy Struktura

Subject + will have been + V-ing + for/since + time
Tasdiqlovchi: I will have been studying for 3 hours.
Inkorlovchi: I will not have been studying for 3 hours.
So'roq: Will I have been studying for 3 hours?

üí°Misollar

EN: By next year, I will have been working here for 5 years.
UZ Kelasi yilga kelib, men bu yerda 5 yil ishlagan bo'laman.
EN: She will have been studying English for 10 years by 2025.
UZ: U 2025 yilga kelib 10 yil ingliz tilini o'rganib kelgan bo'ladi.
EN: We will have been living in this city since 2020.
UZ: Biz 2020 yildan beri shu shaharda yashab kelgan bo'lamiz.

üîëKalit So'zlar

FOR

Vaqt davomiyligi uchun

for 3 hours, for 2 years
SINCE

Boshlanish vaqti uchun

since 2020, since morning
BY

Tugash vaqti uchun

by next year, by 2025
WHEN

Aniq vaqt uchun

when he arrives

üìùAsosiy Qoidalar

1. Vaqt ko'rsatkichlari majburiy

Bu zamon har doim for, since, by kabi so'zlar bilan ishlatiladi.

2. Davomiy harakat

Harakat kelajakda ma'lum paytga kelib davom etib turganini ko'rsatadi.

3. Will have been + V-ing

Formulada will have been so'ngra fe'lga -ing qo'shimchasi qo'shiladi.
