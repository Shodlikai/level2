"THE" - bu ingliz tilida eng ko'p ishlatiladigan artikl bo'lib, aniq, ma'lum yoki avval tilga olingan narsalarni ko'rsatish uchun ishlatiladi.

‚ö°Asosiy Qoidalar

THE + Aniq ism = Ma'lum narsa
Aniq narsa: The book on the table (stoldagi kitob)
Yagona narsa: The sun, the moon (quyosh, oy)
Avval tilga olingan: I saw a cat. The cat was black.

üìùTHE ishlatish holatlari

1. Aniq va ma'lum narsalar

EN: The book I bought yesterday is interesting.
UZ: Kecha sotib olgan kitob qiziqarli.
EN: The car in the garage is mine.
UZ: Garajdagi mashina meniki.

2. Yagona narsalar

EN: The sun rises in the east.
UZ: Quyosh sharqdan chiqadi.
EN: The president visited our city.
UZ: Prezident bizning shahrimizga tashrif buyurdi.

3. Avval tilga olingan narsalar

EN: I saw a dog. The dog was running.
UZ: Men it ko'rdim. It yugurardi.
EN: There's a restaurant here. The restaurant is expensive.
UZ: Bu yerda restoran bor. Restoran qimmat.
