Each, Every, Something Qoidalari

1. Each (har biri)

Each odatda ikki yoki undan ortiq narsalar yoki shaxslarning har birini alohida-alohida ta'kidlash uchun ishlatiladi. Ko'plik otlari yoki olmoshlar bilan ishlatilganda, of bilan keladi.

  • Sonli yoki cheklangan guruhdagi har bir a'zoni nazarda tutishda.
  • Birlik fe'l bilan ishlatiladi.

2. Every (har bir, hamma)

Every odatda uch yoki undan ortiq narsalar yoki shaxslarni guruh sifatida, bir butun holda ifodalash uchun ishlatiladi.

  • Ushbu guruhdagi barcha a'zolarni umumlashtirishda.
  • Birlik fe'l bilan ishlatiladi.
  • Alohida-alohida ma'nosida ishlatilmaydi.

3. Something (nimadir, qandaydir narsa)

Something noaniq olmosh bo'lib, noma'lum, aniq aytilmagan yoki nomlanmagan narsani ifodalash uchun ishlatiladi. Odatda darak gaplarda qo'llaniladi.

  • Noma'lum narsaga ishora qilishda.
  • Fikrlarni, his-tuyg'ularni, va vaziyatlarni ifodalashda.

Each va Every farqlari

Each

Alohida e'tibor + 2 yoki undan ortiq + Birlik fe'l

Misol: Each student received a certificate. (Har bir o'quvchi alohida sertifikat oldi.)

Misol: Each of the books is interesting. (Kitoblarning har biri qiziqarli.)

Misol: There are two doors, one on each side. (Ikkita eshik bor, har bir tarafda bittadan.)

Every

Guruhga e'tibor + 3 yoki undan ortiq + Birlik fe'l

Misol: Every student in the class passed the exam. (Sinfdagi har bir o'quvchi imtihondan o'tdi.)

Misol: We meet every day. (Biz har kuni uchrashamiz.)

Misol: Every country has its own culture. (Har bir davlatning o'z madaniyati bor.)

Something

Noaniq narsa + Darak gaplarda

Misol: I need something to write with. (Yozish uchun nimadir kerak.)

Misol: There is something in your eye. (Ko'zingizda nimadir bor.)

Misol: Did you say something? (Siz nimadir dedingizmi?) - So'roq gaplarda ham bo'lishi mumkin.

Misollar

Each

Each child received a gift.

Har bir bola sovg'a oldi (alohida).

Each of the students has a different opinion.

Talabalarning har birining fikri har xil.

There are four seasons, each with its own beauty.

To'rtta fasl bor, har birining o'z go'zalligi bor.

Every

Every student must participate.

Har bir (barcha) talaba ishtirok etishi kerak.

I go to the gym every morning.

Men har kuni ertalab sport zaliga boraman.

Every person needs to feel loved.

Har bir inson sevilganini his qilishi kerak.

Something

I heard something strange.

G'alati nimadir eshitdim.

Do you want something to drink?

Ichishga nimadir xohlaysizmi?

There is something important I need to tell you.

Sizga aytadigan muhim nimadir bor.
