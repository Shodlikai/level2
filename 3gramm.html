Future Simple Qoidalari

1. Future Simple nima?

Future Simple (Kelasi oddiy zamon) kelajakda sodir bo'ladigan voqea yoki harakatni ifodalash uchun ishlatiladi. Bu zamon ko'pincha oldindan rejalashtirilmagan, spontan qarorlar yoki bashoratlarni ifodalaydi.

2. Qachon ishlatiladi?

  • Spontan qarorlar: Gapirayotgan paytda qabul qilingan qarorlar uchun. Masalan: "The phone is ringing. I will answer it." (Telefon jiringlayapti. Men javob beraman.)
  • Bashoratlar: Odatda "think", "believe", "probably" kabi so'zlar bilan birga ishlatiladigan subyektiv bashoratlar uchun. Masalan: "I think it will rain tomorrow." (Menimcha, ertaga yomg'ir yog'adi.)
  • Vadalashuvlar va takliflar: Kimdirga va'da berish, taklif qilish yoki biror narsa qilishga rozi bo'lish uchun. Masalan: "I will help you with your homework." (Men senga uy vazifangda yordam beraman.)
  • Shartli gaplar (Type 1): Hozirgi yoki kelajakda amalga oshishi mumkin bo'lgan harakatlar haqida gapirganda. Masalan: "If it rains, we will stay at home." (Agar yomg'ir yog'sa, biz uyda qolamiz.)

3. Vaqt ko'rsatkichlari (Keywords)

tomorrow next week/month/year in 2050 soon later in a few days I think / I believe probably / perhaps

Future Simple Strukturalari

Darak gap (+)

Subject + will + V1 (Base form) + Object

Misol: She will visit us next week.

Tarjima: U keyingi hafta bizni ziyorat qiladi.

Inkor gap (-)

Subject + will not (won't) + V1 (Base form) + Object

Misol: They will not (won't) come to the party.

Tarjima: Ular ziyofatga kelishmaydi.

So'roq gap (?)

Will + Subject + V1 (Base form) + Object + ?

Misol: Will you help me with this?

Tarjima: Sen menga bu ishda yordam berasanmi?

Maxsus So'roq gaplar (Wh-Questions)

Future Simple zamonida ma'lumot olish uchun Wh-so'zlari (Who, What, Where, When, Why, How) ishlatiladi. Ular gap boshiga joylashtirilib, standart so'roq gap tuzilmasi saqlanadi.

Wh-Question Struktura

Wh-Word + will + Subject + V1 (Base form) + ?

Future Simple va Boshqa Zamonlar Farqi

Future Simple ko'pincha kelajakni ifodalovchi boshqa zamonlar (Future Continuous, Future Perfect, Present Continuous, Going to) bilan chalkashtiriladi. Ularning asosiy farqlari quyidagilardir:

Future Simple vs. Be Going To

Will (Future Simple) oldindan rejalashtirilmagan, spontan qarorlar yoki shaxsiy bashoratlar uchun ishlatiladi.

Be Going To oldindan qilingan rejalar yoki aniq dalillarga asoslangan bashoratlar uchun ishlatiladi.

  • Will: "Iâ€™m thirsty. I will buy a drink." (Chaqqon qaror)
  • Going To: "I bought a ticket. I am going to fly to London tomorrow." (Reja)

Future Simple vs. Present Continuous

Present Continuous kelajak uchun belgilangan, qat'iy reja (odatda uchrashuv, sayohat kabi) uchun ishlatiladi.

  • Present Continuous: "We are meeting at 7 PM tonight." (Aniqlangan uchrashuv)
  • Future Simple: "I will see him soon." (Umumiy bashorat/va'da)
