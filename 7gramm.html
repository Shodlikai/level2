Future Continuous Tense va "Shall" Qoidalari

Future Continuous Tense (Kelajak Davomiy Zamon)

Future Continuous Tense kelajakda aniq belgilangan vaqtda sodir bo‘ladigan va davom etayotgan harakatni ifodalash uchun ishlatiladi. Asosiy tuzilma: Subject + will be + V-ing.

Future Continuous Qo'llanilishi

  • Kelajakda aniq vaqtda davom etuvchi harakat: Kelajakda belgilangan vaqtda nima bo‘layotganini ifodalash.

    Misol: "At 8 PM tonight, I will be watching a movie." (Bugun kechki soat 8 da men film tomosha qilayotgan bo'laman.)

  • To‘xtatilgan harakat: Boshqa qisqa harakat tomonidan to‘xtatilgan davomiy harakat.

    Misol: "I will be sleeping when you arrive." (Sen yetib kelganingda, men uxlayotgan bo'laman.)

  • Muloyim so‘roqlar: Kelajakdagi rejalar haqida muloyimlik bilan so‘rash.

    Misol: "Will you be attending the meeting?" (Siz yig'ilishda qatnashayotgan bo'lasizmi?)

Future Continuous vs. Future Simple Farqi

Future Continuous davom etuvchi jarayonga urg'u beradi, Future Simple esa kelajakdagi bir martalik harakat yoki qarorni ifodalaydi.

  • Future Simple: *I will cook dinner.* (Kechki ovqat pishiraman. - Bir martalik harakat.)
  • Future Continuous: *I will be cooking dinner at 6 PM.* (Soat 6 da men kechki ovqat pishirayotgan bo'laman. - Davom etuvchi jarayon.)

"Shall I?" va "Shall we?" (Takliflar va Maslahatlar)

"Shall" Future Continuous zamoni bilan bog'liq emas. U faqat **I** va **We** olmoshlari bilan taklif va maslahat uchun ishlatiladi. Bu muloyim shakl.

  • Shall I?: Biror kishi uchun yordam taklif qilish.

    Misol: "Shall I open the window?" (Derazani ochsammi?)

  • Shall we?: Birgalikda biror ish qilishni taklif qilish.

    Misol: "Shall we go to the park?" (Parkka borsakmi?)

Istisnolar va To'g'ri Foydalanish

1. Stative Fe'llar (Holat fe'llari)

Future Continuous odatda **stative fe'llar** (know, like, understand, believe, want, have kabi) bilan ishlatilmaydi. Ular bilan Future Simple ishlatiladi.

Noto‘g‘ri: I will be knowing the answer.

To‘g‘ri: I will know the answer.

2. "Shall" va "Will" o'rtasidagi farq

"Will" kelajakdagi qarorni, "Shall I/we" esa faqat taklifni bildiradi.

Future Continuous Strukturalari

Darak gap (+)

Subject + will be + V-ing + Object

Misol: They will be playing football at 5 PM.

Inkor gap (-)

Subject + will not (won't) be + V-ing + Object

Misol: She won't be watching TV when we arrive.

So'roq gap (?)

Will + Subject + be + V-ing + Object + ?

Misol: Will they be waiting for us?

Shall I? / Shall we?

Shall + I / We + V1 + Object + ?

Misol: Shall I open the door? (Taklif)

Misol: Shall we go now? (Taklif)
