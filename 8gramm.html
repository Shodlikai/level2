Present Perfect Tense: To'liq Tushuntirish

Hozirgi Tugallangan Zamon (Present Perfect) va uning kalit so'zlarini o'rganing!

Present Perfect Tense Nima?

Present Perfect (Hozirgi Tugallangan Zamon) o'tmishda sodir bo'lgan, ammo hozirgi paytga bog'liq bo'lgan harakatlar yoki holatlarni ifodalash uchun ishlatiladi. Bu zamon harakatning qachon sodir bo'lganidan ko'ra, uning hozirgi paytdagi natijasi yoki tajribasiga ko'proq e'tibor beradi.

Eslatma: Agar harakatning aniq vaqti (yesterday, last week, 2010, etc.) ko'rsatilsa, odatda Present Perfect o'rniga Simple Past ishlatiladi.

Strukturalar

Present Perfect Tense ning gap tuzilishi quyidagicha:

Darak Gap (Affirmative)

Ega (Subject) + have / has + Fe'lning 3-shakli (Past Participle - Verb 3)

Misol: I have finished my homework. (Men uy vazifamni tugatdim.)

Misol: She has visited Paris. (U Parijda bo'lgan.)

Inkor Gap (Negative)

Ega (Subject) + have not / has not (yoki haven't / hasn't) + Fe'lning 3-shakli

Misol: We haven't seen that movie. (Biz bu filmni ko'rmadik.)

Misol: He hasn't arrived yet. (U hali kelmadi.)

So'roq Gap (Interrogative)

Have / Has + Ega (Subject) + Fe'lning 3-shakli?

Misol: Have you ever eaten sushi? (Siz hech sushi yeganmisiz?)

Misol: Has she found her keys? (U kalitlarini topdimi?)

Asosiy Ishlatilish Holatlari

1. Harakatning Natijasi Hozirgi Paytga Tegishli

O'tmishda sodir bo'lgan harakatning hozirgi paytdagi natijasi yoki oqibati muhim bo'lsa.

Misol: I have lost my keys. (Men kalitlarimni yo'qotdim. - Hozirda ularni topa olmayapman.)

2. Hayotiy Tajriba (Experience)

Hozirgi paytgacha hayotingizda sodir bo'lgan tajribalar haqida gapirganda. Ko'pincha "ever" (biron marta) va "never" (hech qachon) bilan ishlatiladi.

Misol: She has never seen a real elephant. (U hech qachon haqiqiy filni ko'rmagan.)

Misol: Have you ever been to London? (Siz hech Londonda bo'lganmisiz?)

3. Yaqinda Tugallangan Harakatlar

Juda yaqinda tugallangan harakatlar uchun. Ko'pincha "just" (hozirgina), "already" (allaqachon), "yet" (hali, hali emas) bilan ishlatiladi.

Misol: He has just arrived. (U hozirgina yetib keldi.)

Misol: They have already finished dinner. (Ular allaqachon kechki ovqatni tugatishdi.)

Misol: I haven't done it yet. (Men buni hali qilmadim.)

4. O'tmishda Boshlanib, Hozirgacha Davom Etayotgan Harakatlar

Bu holatda "Since" va "For" kalit so'zlari juda muhimdir.

Misol: We have lived here since 2015. (Biz bu yerda 2015 yildan beri yashayapmiz.)

Misol: She has worked there for five years. (U besh yildan beri u yerda ishlaydi.)

"Since" va "For": Batafsil Tushuntirish

Present Perfect zamoni bilan "Since" va "For" kalit so'zlari juda ko'p ishlatiladi. Ular harakatning qachon boshlanganini yoki qancha vaqt davom etganini aniqlashga yordam beradi. Ularning farqini tushunish bu zamonni to'g'ri ishlatish uchun juda muhimdir.

"Since" (dan beri)

"Since" harakatning **qachon boshlanganini (boshlanish nuqtasini)** bildiradi. U o'tmishdagi aniq bir vaqtni yoki voqeani ko'rsatadi.

Qoidalar:

1. Aniq Boshlanish Nuqtasi

"Since" dan keyin o'tmishdagi aniq bir vaqt (yil, sana, soat) yoki voqea keladi.

Misol: I have lived here since 2010. (Men bu yerda 2010 yildan beri yashayapman.)

Misol: She has been studying since 9 o'clock. (U soat 9 dan beri o'qiyapti.)

Misol: We haven't seen him since last week. (Biz uni o'tgan haftadan beri ko'rmadik.)

2. Vaqt Ko'rsatuvchi Gaplar bilan

"Since" dan keyin o'tmishda boshlangan boshqa bir gap kelishi mumkin.

Misol: He has worked here since he graduated from university. (U universitetni tugatganidan beri shu yerda ishlaydi.)

Misol: They haven't visited us since they moved to London. (Ular Londonga ko'chib o'tganlaridan beri bizni ko'rgani kelmadi.)

Noto'g'ri Ishlatilishga Misollar:

Xato: I have been here since three hours. (Bu yerda muddat berilgan, nuqta emas)

To'g'ri: I have been here for three hours.

Xato: She has studied since a long time. (Bu aniq boshlanish nuqtasi emas)

To'g'ri: She has studied for a long time.

"For" (davomida)

"For" harakatning **qancha vaqt davom etganini (muddatini)** bildiradi. U vaqt oralig'ini ko'rsatadi.

Qoidalar:

1. Vaqt Oralig'i

"For" dan keyin ma'lum bir vaqt oralig'i (kunlar, haftalar, oylar, yillar, daqiqalar, soatlar) keladi.

Misol: I have lived here for 15 years. (Men bu yerda 15 yildan beri yashayapman.)

Misol: She has been studying for two hours. (U ikki soatdan beri o'qiyapti.)

Misol: We haven't seen him for a long time. (Biz uni uzoq vaqtdan beri ko'rmadik.)

2. Barcha Zamonlarda Ishlatilishi

"For" nafaqat Present Perfect, balki boshqa zamonlarda ham muddatni ifodalash uchun ishlatilishi mumkin (masalan, Simple Past, Future Simple).

Misol: I studied for three hours yesterday. (Men kecha uch soat o'qidim. - Simple Past)

Misol: He will be here for a week. (U bir hafta shu yerda bo'ladi. - Future Simple)

Noto'g'ri Ishlatilishga Misollar:

Xato: I have been here for 2010. (Bu muddat emas, balki boshlanish nuqtasi)

To'g'ri: I have been here since 2010.

Xato: She has worked for last Monday. (Bu aniq boshlanish nuqtasi)

To'g'ri: She has worked since last Monday.

Xususiyat Since For
Ma'nosi -dan beri (boshlanish nuqtasi) davomida (muddat)
Dan keyin keladi Aniq vaqt / voqea Vaqt oralig'i (son + vaqt birligi)
Misollar since 2005, since May, since last week, since I was a child for 5 years, for 3 months, for two days, for a long time

Mashqlar

1. Gapni To'ldiring (Fe'lning 3-shakli)

Qavs ichidagi fe'lni Present Perfect zamonida to'g'ri shaklda yozing.

1. I _____ (finish) my work already.

2. She _____ (not / see) him since last year.

3. They _____ (live) in London for five years.

4. _____ you ever _____ (eat) sushi?

2. "Since" yoki "For" ni Tanlang

Har bir gap uchun "Since" yoki "For" dan to'g'risini tanlang.

5. He has worked here _____ 2018.

6. We have been waiting _____ two hours.

7. I haven't seen her _____ she moved to New York.

3. Gapni Qayta Tartiblang

So'zlardan to'g'ri Present Perfect gapini tuzing.

8. finished / already / has / she / her homework.

finished already has she her homework

9. never / tried / I / have / sushi.

never tried I have sushi
