Irregular Adjectives: Tushuntirish

Noto'g'ri Sifatlarni (Irregular Adjectives) oson o'rganing!

Nima uchun Noto'g'ri Sifatlar (Irregular Adjectives)?

Ingliz tilidagi sifatlarning ko'pchiligi qiyosiy (comparative) va orttirma (superlative) darajalarini -er va -est qo'shimchalarini qo'shish yoki "more" va "most" so'zlarini ishlatish orqali hosil qiladi. Biroq, ba'zi sifatlar mavjudki, ular ushbu qoidalarga bo'ysunmaydi va butunlay boshqacha shakllarga ega bo'ladi. Ular "Irregular Adjectives" (Noto'g'ri Sifatlar) deb ataladi. Keling, ularni o'rganamiz!

Asosiy Tushuncha 

1. Shakl O'zgarishi

Oddiy qoidalardan farqli o'laroq, bu sifatlarning qiyosiy va orttirma darajalari butunlay boshqacha so'zlar bo'lishi mumkin.

Misol: Good (Yaxshi)  Better (Yaxshiroq)  Best (Eng yaxshi)

Misol: Bad (Yomon)  Worse (Yomonroq)  Worst (Eng yomon)

2. Yodlash Zarurati

Bu sifatlar uchun ma'lum bir qoida yo'q, shuning uchun ularni yodlab olish muhimdir. Ular ingliz tilida juda tez-tez ishlatiladi.

Misol: Far (Uzoq)  Farther/Further (Uzoqroq)  Farthest/Furthest (Eng uzoq)

Strukturalar (Ishlatilishi) 

Irregular Adjectives xuddi boshqa sifatlar kabi ishlatiladi, faqat ularning qiyosiy va orttirma shakllari boshqacha bo'ladi.

Asosiy Daraja (Positive Degree)

Ega + Fe'l + Sifat + Boshqa qism

Misol: This is a good book.

Qiyosiy Daraja (Comparative Degree)

Ega + Fe'l + Noto'g'ri sifatning qiyosiy shakli + than + Nima bilan qiyoslanyapti

Misol: This book is better than that one.

Orttirma Daraja (Superlative Degree)

Ega + Fe'l + the + Noto'g'ri sifatning orttirma shakli + Boshqa qism

Misol: This is the best book I've ever read.

Keng Tarqalgan Noto'g'ri Sifatlar Ro'yxati 

Quyida ingliz tilidagi eng ko'p ishlatiladigan noto'g'ri sifatlar ro'yxati keltirilgan:

Positive Degree Comparative Degree Superlative Degree
good better best
bad worse worst
far farther / further farthest / furthest
little less least
much / many more most
old older / elder oldest / eldest
late later / latter latest / last
near nearer nearest / next
fore former foremost / first
in inner inmost / innermost
out outer / utter outmost / uttermost / utmost
up upper upmost / uppermost

Izoh: "Farther" va "farthest" asosan jismoniy masofa uchun ishlatiladi, "further" va "furthest" esa mavhumroq (qo'shimcha, keyingi) ma'nolar uchun ham ishlatilishi mumkin.

"Elder" va "eldest" odatda oila a'zolari orasida (aka-uka, opa-singil) yoshni bildirish uchun ishlatiladi. "Older" va "oldest" har qanday shaxs yoki narsa uchun ishlatiladi.

"Later" va "latest" vaqtga tegishli. "Latter" - ikkita narsadan ikkinchisi. "Last" - oxirgisi (tartibda).

Misollar 

This is the best movie I've seen.

(Best - good ning superlativi)

My headache is worse today.

(Worse - bad ning comparativi)

London is farther than Paris from here.

(Farther - far ning comparativi, masofa)

I need further information.

(Further - far ning comparativi, qo'shimcha)

He has less money than I do.

(Less - little ning comparativi)

She has the most experience in the team.

(Most - much/many ning superlativi)

My elder brother lives in Canada.

(Elder - old ning comparativi, oila)

What's the latest news?

(Latest - late ning superlativi, vaqt)
